SecPriv: Security and Privacy at EPFL

This is the homepage of the Security and Privacy group in the School of Computer and Communication Sciences (IC) at EPFL in Lausanne, Switzerland.

EPFL has a long history in cryptography and systems with a recent focus on privacy, software security, and cryptographic analysis. The labs working in security and privacy are closely connected and often collaborate on research projects --- or for social outings and fun interactions.

If you'd like to join the IC school as a graduate student, apply to our PhD program or our master program, especially the Master in Cyber Security could be of interest. If you're a recent PhD graduate, we're also looking for PostDocs.
